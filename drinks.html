Bramble

Ingredients

  • 5cl gin
  • 2cl blackberry liqueur
  • 3cl lemon juice
  • 2cl syrup
  • 1 blackberry

Making Of

  1. Pour gin, lemon juice and syrup in a shaker
  2. Fill the shaker with ice and shake until cold
  3. Fill a glass with crushed ice and strain the drink
  4. Top it off with blackberry liqueur
  5. Garnish with blackberry and lemon wedge

Caipirhina

Ingredients

  • 4-6cl cachaca
  • 0.5-1 lime
  • 1.5-2cl sugar

Making Of

  1. Cut off the ends of the limes and half it lenghtwise
  2. Take away the white part on the lime
  3. Cut every half in four and put preferred amount into a glas
  4. Add sugar and muddle the sugar with the lime
  5. Fill the glass with ice, add cachaca and stir

Strawberry Mojito

Ingredients

  • 4cl white rum
  • 3cl lime juice
  • 3cl syrup
  • 4cl soda
  • mint
  • strawberries

Making Of

  1. Muddle 2-3 strawberries in a high-ball glass
  2. Add lime juice and syrup
  3. Take a bundle of mint and put in the glass, carefully tap with a muddle
  4. Pour in rum and fill a little more than half the glass with ice and stir
  5. Add soda and fill the rest up with ice
  6. Garnish with mint and strawberries

Irish Coffee

Ingredients

  • 3cl irish whiskey
  • 1-2tsp sugar
  • 9cl coffee
  • 5cl cream

Making Of

  1. Add sugar, whiskey and coffee (leave about 1-1.5cm of the glass for the cream)
  2. Stir until the sugar has dissolved
  3. With a spoon, add the cream on top
  4. Garnish with nutmeg or grated chocolate

Tip: Heat the glass with hot water beforhand.

Kaffe Karlsson

Ingredients

  • 3cl baileys
  • 3cl orange liqueur (triple sec)
  • 15cl coffee
  • cream

Making Of

  1. Pour baileys and orange liqueur in a glas
  2. Add the coffee, leave a few cm for cream
  3. With a spoon, add the lightly whipped cream on top
  4. Garnish with chocolate
